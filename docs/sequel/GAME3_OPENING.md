# Game 3 — Opening Town *(design sketch)*

> **Era:** Commodity Era · **Format:** 2D · **Status:** not implemented

> Sacred pillars: [`../SAGA.md`](../SAGA.md) §0 · Foreshadow map: [`../FORESHADOWING.md`](../FORESHADOWING.md)

## Design goal (first ten minutes)

Teach three facts through play, not lecture:

1. **Anula** is money — you need it for rent and food.
2. **Anula** is magic — spending it casts wards / powers relays.
3. **Flesh** echoes exist — garrison chant off the market square; rumor of Red Litany in the pits.

Player fantasy: **dock clerk or mine courier** in a company town — not the Messenger, but
living under their face on a broadsheet.

---

## Working town: **Splitpath Station**

A surveyed rail-head where **Ring Road** freight meets **crystal-line** relays — built
on the same crossing KoN players know, now a mapped hub.

| Zone | Gameplay | Story |
|---|---|---|
| **Market board** | See Anula buy/sell prices; quest hook | Prices updated by line from Ofelos |
| **Company house** | Rent due in 3 in-game days | Boss holds wages in crystal scrip |
| **Relay tower** | Optional: spend shard to read broadsheet | Messenger legend + war debt ads |
| **Garrison post** | Flesh-Wardens rotate; overhear chant | Licensed vs cult contrast |
| **Mine road** (exit north) | First dungeon / job route | Unsafe pit; dust-runners |

---

## Opening beat (script spine)

1. **Wake** — shift bell; landlord knocks; rent = 12 graded shards.
2. **Walk market** — board shows Anula up after Sylvian elder protest (line news).
3. **Job offer** — courier packet to relay tower; pays 8 shards (not enough alone).
4. **Choice** — overtime mine run (danger) vs pawning heirloom vs ward-skip (dark).
5. **Inciting incident** — chant riot at garrison; whispers of **Red Litany** in the pit.
6. **Hook** — broadsheet: living Messenger denies saint reliquary; PC recognizes the name from family lore / Cataracta diaspora.
