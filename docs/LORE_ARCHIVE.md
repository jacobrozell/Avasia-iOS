## 1. Canon merged from found docs (2026-06-15)

### 1.1 The schism fable (Kaefden vs Agromanian)

**Status:** Extended backstory — supplements the Oceandale gate-guard speech in
KoN; does not replace verbatim in-game lines.

Long ago all of Avasia united under **King Kaefden** and the royal family at
**Aylova**. The king had two sons. The **younger son** was not next in line — his
**older brother** was heir. After years of jealousy and greed, the younger son
started an **underground movement** to dethrone his father. The movement went
public and turned violent.

The king and the heir suppressed the outbreak with force. The king loved his son
but could not appear weak; he **banished** him from all Kaefden lands. The son fled
**as far west** as he could from Aylova. Followers of the movement went with him,
and new recruits joined from neighboring colonies — **Mages, Druids, and Humans**.

They named their faction after the son's **middle name: Agroman**.

The original king and both brothers are long dead, but **Aylova's throne remains
in the Kaefden bloodline** (→ Kaefden IV in the Age-era games).

**In-game compression (KoN gate guard):** *"youngest son… thirsted for power…
banished… younger brother built the Agromanian faction."* The fable adds middle
name, underground movement, and westward flight.

### 1.2 Oceandale

**Status:** Canon geography / history.

- Began as a **fishing colony** for work across Avasia.
- Population grew until Kaefden could no longer treat it as a mere colony — it
  joined the **Kaefden faction** formally.
- Its fishing economy and coastal layout made it the ideal place for Agromanian
  **warning attacks** (KoN opens *"last week"* after such a strike).

---

### 1.3 Silvarium

**Status:** Canon — explains KoN Great Tree, blood seal, Anula, and marksman.

**Founding myth**
- **Suformin**, Goddess of the Hunt, led hunters to the **Great Tree**.
- When Suformin ascended, she entrusted her **dagger** to one worthy man; he
  founded the **elder system** still used in Silvarium.

**Joining Kaefden**
- After decades on the tree, Silvarium officially joined the Kaefden faction.
- **Mage gift:** **Stone-Bend** (Stonebend) to help mine and forge hunting arms.
  Elders restricted who could learn it, then **locked it** behind a seal whose
  only key was **mage blood** (→ Floor 4 blood ritual in KoN).
- **Druid gift:** cartloads of **Anula** (blue crystals) — ties Cataracta's
  garden fountain to Sylvian shrines (SoC Act I / elders).

**After Oceandale (KoN timeline)**
- Elders decided most Sylvians should **migrate north**, away from the fighting.
- Roughly **~80% left**; **~20% stayed** as homeland defenders (→ Sylvian
  marksman in KoN: *"A few of us decided to stay"*).
- Departing elders **sealed** the Stonebend chamber at the tree top (marksman +
  priestess + librarian dialogue in KoN).

**SoC note:** Elders who hold sway over **Ofelos** (Act IV) are the political
 heirs of this system — Elder Venna in iOS-authored prose.

### 1.4 Mountain cave (KoN)

**Status:** Canon flavor — explains corpses and old architecture.

Before the Old Mages hid **Inflame** there, the cave served as a **jail and
punishment center** for thieves, murderers, and anarchists. The pink-crystal
cavern and branching passages fit a repurposed prison turned tomb.

---

### 1.5 Cataracta elk feast (story seed)

**Status:** Not in shipped games — **neutral / side-story seed**.

Annual feast honoring the **First King of Cataracta**; elk harvest determines
next year's prosperity. Draft opens with a bad hunting year (NORTH / WEST woods).
Could become a SoC prequel vignette, game-3 side quest, or `fiction/` short story.

## 2. Alternate design — not Age-era canon

### 2.1 Intro adventure: scout patrol & alignment fork

**Status:** **Archived idea** — conflicts with shipped KoN / SoC openings.

Draft pitch:
- Intro adventure determines **good vs bad alignment** (white hat / black hat).
- Protagonist on **scout patrol** discovers an Agromanian army led by **Vashirr**.
- Player chooses: **report to Kaefden** or **walk with Vashirr** — framed as
  almost a **religious experience** (abandoning one's whole life).

**Why not current canon:** KoN opens with an **amnesiac mage on Oceandale beach**.
SoC opens with a **named Druid volunteer** in Cataracta. Neither uses an alignment
fork at scout patrol.

**Future use:** Game 3+ anthology story, "Story #0" in a faction-point system, or
a **what-if** Vashirr POV path — not a retcon of games 1–2.

### 2.2 Faction points, story unlocks, Ring Passes

**Status:** **Future meta-system** (not implemented).

Draft economy:
- **Money** → gear, Ring Passes (excuse one unclosed ring per day?)
- **Faction points** → unlock numbered "Story Adventures"
  - Good path: #1 @ 500 FP, #2 @ 1000, …
  - Bad path: #1 @ 500 (e.g. *Walking with Vashirr*)
  - Neutral: many small everyday stories per alignment

**Relation to saga:** Could layer on **post–Age era** or a live-service anthology
app — separate from KoN/SoC critical paths. See [`SAGA.md`](SAGA.md) §3.
