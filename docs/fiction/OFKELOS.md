## Key institutions

### Observer Corps

Neutral witnesses sent to foreign crises — **record, do not act** (until policy
changes). Cassan (II-M06) is senior observer. Reports are admissible in Charter
Bridge courts.

### Blade clause

Ofelos will not march until **Kaefden's Blade** — relic of the **first king** at
Varatro Falls — is presented. Not worship: **legal proof** that coalition serves
civic Avasia, not mage crown alone.

### Line House (post–Age)

After Anula commodification, Ofelos runs **crystal-line** relays — news and prices
faster than horses. Sylvian elders protest enclosure via line petitions (III-M06).

## Voice & tone

Measured, contractual, never flowery. Ofelos speaks in **if/then** clauses.
Emotion is admitted only in Memorial Walk scenes.

**Sample:** *"Neutrality is not absence of judgment. It is delayed execution of judgment until the terms are met."*
