Synopsis

Tavi sells ungraded Anula dust scraped from mine carts — enough to eat, not
enough to be safe. Arrest means company indenture; escape means pit without lamp.
Player choice in game 3 branches; prose ends unclean.

Scene beats

  1. Scrape — dust on fingers; blue under nails.
  2. Sale — black market stall; haggling in shards.
  3. Patrol — Wardens not cruel, still lawful.
  4. Fork — help Tavi or report (two endings).
  5. Coda — either indenture contract or burned hovel — never fairy tale.

Theme

Economy cruelty — children priced.

Tone

Bitter realism.
