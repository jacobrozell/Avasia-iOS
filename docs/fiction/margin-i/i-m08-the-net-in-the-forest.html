I-M08 — The Net in the Forest

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN forest trap · Agromanian roadblocks
POV Third limited — Agromanian scout Pell
Length target 3,500 words
When KoN — same week as I-M07
Cast Pell; sergeant; unseen mage victim

Synopsis

Pell strings the net where the western road narrows. Sergeant wants prisoners
for trade; Pell thought they were hunting deserters. When the trap catches a mage,
argument breaks out: asset or corpse? Pell follows orders and still feels the schism
in his chest — brotherhood oath vs Kaefden hate.

Theme

Enemy interiority without redemption.

Tone

Gray, military. No Vashirr on-page.

Connections

I-M07 · I-M01 Corren on road.

Key dialogue

  • Sergeant: "A live mage buys more than a dead patriot."
  • Pell: "Agroman meant loyalty. This feels like butchery."
