Synopsis

The church burns but Edda saved the tithe ledger: decades of Oceandale payments
to Aylova — proof the colony was civic Kaefden, not tower charity. She argues
with a raider that you cannot sack a city that paid its taxes; he burns the pews
anyway. The ledger survives; the faith does not.

Scene beats

  1. Fire — save the book, not the altar.
  2. Ledger read — names, sums, oaths.
  3. Confrontation — civic anchor vs sword.
  4. Survivors — fishers shelter in crypt.
  5. Coda — Edda brings ledger to gate guard; KoN graveyard church flavor.

Theme

Civic anchor vs tower — Oceandale deserved to stand.
