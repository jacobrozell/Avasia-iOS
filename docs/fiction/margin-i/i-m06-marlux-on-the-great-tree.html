Synopsis

Marlux's father ships north with the elders' migration. On the Great Tree: floor 1
fear, floor 2 butcher's resentment of the mage deal, floor 3 priestess who says
the meaning of life is 42 and means it as kindness. Marlux does not understand
the blood seal upstairs; he understands absence.

  5. Coda — Marlux draws the tree in dust; father not in the picture.
