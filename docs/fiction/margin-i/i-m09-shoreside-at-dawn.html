I-M09 — Shoreside at Dawn

Synopsis

Brann runs nets at dawn and watches mages fall from the sky like broken birds.
Ash stops in a clean arc west of the magehouse — he will not cross the humming
line. He sells fish to the gate guard anyway; war does not cancel hunger. He
tells the guard: "Your blue robes wash up empty-eyed. Something old is waiting
for one of them."

Scene beats

  1. Dawn haul — empty nets; sky-splash overnight.
  2. Ward arc — torches slide off; Brann tests with a thrown ember.
  3. Guard barter — fish for rumors; scatter count.
  4. Figure on beach — Brann sees but does not approach (player, unnamed).
  5. Coda — sells anyway; superstition and commerce coexist.

Theme

Ground witnesses sky; civic life persists.

Tone

Quiet, coastal. One magefall image.
