Synopsis

Eighty percent of Sylvians went north; Tenn stayed to defend homeland. He finds
a mage in a net trap — Agromanian work — and cuts him free knowing elders may
call it betrayal. He says "Kaefden to Kaefden" not as slogan but as choice:
solidarity across species when the schism says otherwise.

Scene beats

  1. Patrol — empty forest; migration guilt.
  2. Trap — mage struggling; Tenn hesitates.
  3. Cut — frees player; hands sword direction.
  4. Elder scout — confrontation; Tenn does not repent.
  5. Coda — mage gone east; Tenn reloads; net burned.

Theme

Cross-faction loyalty as daily risk.
