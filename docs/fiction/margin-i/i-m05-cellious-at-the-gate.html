I-M05 — Cellious at the Gate

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN ForestRooms / Cataracta gate denial
POV Third limited — Cellious, chief hunter
Length target 3,500 words
When KoN timeline — day player is turned away
Cast Cellious; King of Cataracta (off-stage); messenger; mage at gate

Synopsis

A mage in blue robes asks entry; orders say deny everyone while Kimious
negotiates isolation. Cellious follows the law and hates it. He sees the mage's
eyes — lost, not hostile — and still speaks the refusal. That night he writes
Kimious a letter he does not send: "The war will come to our gate because we
would not open it."

Scene beats

  1. Dawn orders — gate sealed; paranoia in the court.
  2. Mage arrives — polite; asks for lantern road.
  3. Refusal — formal apology; mage leaves east.
  4. Wolf scare — same day; Cellious apologizes to empty road.
  5. Night — unsent letter; fox watched from the treeline (Dentros, unnamed).

Theme

Duty without wisdom — prelude to Cataracta's isolation cost.

Tone

Earnest, restrained.
