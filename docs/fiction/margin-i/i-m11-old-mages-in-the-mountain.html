Synopsis

The mountain cave was a prison before it was a vault. Three Old Mages hide
Inflame, Stonebend, and Levitate in separate trials — not for wisdom but because
they could not agree who deserved legacy. Jealousy kills one; the survivors seal
the cave and lie: "Only the worthy."

Scene beats

  1. Prison history — chains under the forge.
  2. Council of three — split the spells.
  3. Trial design — puzzles as ego.
  4. Murder — rival sabotages flame trial.
  5. Seal — worthy-blood lie born.
  6. Coda — KoN player enters unaware.
