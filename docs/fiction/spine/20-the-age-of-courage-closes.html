Story 20 — The Age of Courage Closes

Field Value
Canon anchor SoC — Age epilogue (SoCAgeEpilogueRoom.swift)
POV Second person present
Length target 5,000 words
Cast Kaefden IV; Thekia; survivors; optional ruins pilgrimage

Synopsis: Weeks later, Aylova's gates. A memorial stone lists Kimious,
Dentros, and thousands unnamed. Thekia lights blue crystal at the base. Kaefden
honors you by name — the druid who delivered Vashirr's threat and held
Oceandale ridge. If you honored Dentros at court, the king says it mattered. The
Druid Legion is reborn in survivors. Optionally, you walk Cataracta's ruins and
close the circle. The Age era ends; a new era will play in a different form.

Scene beats:
1. Victorious column enters Aylova.
2. Memorial — Thekia's line: "We carry them forward."
3. Kaefden's speech — Cataracta, Vashirr, Blade, Ofelos.
4. Personal honor — player named.
5. Crowd — Dentros, Kimious, ashes carried to joy.
6. Optional — Cataracta ruins revisit.
7. Coda — "The Age of Courage closes."

Theme: Victory is memorial, not erasure. Survivors carry the dead forward.

Tone: Earnest closure. Series end for text era.

Connections: Story 10 (peace before fire); Story 12 (Dentros honored); SAGA.md
game 3 setup.
