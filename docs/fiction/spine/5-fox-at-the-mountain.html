Story 5 — Fox at the Mountain

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN — Dentros, mountain, druid gate
POV Second person present
Length target 4,500 words
Cast Dentros; Cellious; druid hunters; black wolf

Synopsis: A red fox becomes a man with cherry-red hair and gives you a lantern.
He sends you toward the cave where Old Mages hid their secrets. At Cataracta's
stone gate, Sylvian Anula gleams in the masonry and hunters shapeshift — man
to wolf — until you prove you are a mage. Chief Cellious turns you away: their
king fears spies. You never enter the city you will one day see burned.

Scene beats:
1. Fox encounter — transformation reveal.
2. Lantern gift — cave foreshadowed.
3. Blue crystal gate — six hunters.
4. Wolf scare — proof of magehood (ears, Levitate, Stonebend if earned early).
5. Cellious apology — class structure explained; entry denied.

Theme: Allies behind closed borders. Shapeshifting as trust problem.

Tone: Wonder + tension. First druid warmth.

Connections: Story 9 (Dentros recruiter); Story 10 (Cataracta destroyed);
Character bible — Dentros spans both games.
