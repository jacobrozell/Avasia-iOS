Story 15 — What the Elders Require

Field Value
Canon anchor SoC Act IV — Aylova war camp, Silvarium elders
POV Second person present
Length target 5,000 words
Cast Thekia (quartermaster); Sylvian elders; coalition officers

Synopsis: War camp at Aylova. Thekia provisions you. Sylvian elders will not
speak for Ofelos without Kaefden's Blade of Courage from Varatro Falls — the
first king's tomb. Envoys ride; you march north with the army. Neutrality has a
price: symbols, not speeches.

Scene beats:
1. War camp muster — briefing, supplies.
2. Elders' hall — conditional alliance.
3. Quest accepted — Varatro Falls.
4. Column north — coalition texture (mages, druids, humans).

Theme: Unity requires proof, not rhetoric.

Tone: Military fantasy. Elders echo Story 6 librarian — blood and worth.

Connections: Story 6 (Silvarium); Story 16 (Blade recovery).
