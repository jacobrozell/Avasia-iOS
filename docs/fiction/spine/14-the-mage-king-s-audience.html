Story 14 — The Mage-King's Audience

Field Value
Canon anchor SoC Act III — throne room (SoCThroneRoom.swift)
POV Second person present (optional Kaefden interlude in third)
Length target 6,000 words
Cast Kaefden IV; Thekia; court

Synopsis: Blue crystal light — same hue as Anula's fountain. Kaefden IV has
mage ears and seven years of grief in his eyes. You choose to honor Dentros first
or report facts; you deliver Vashirr's threat word for word. The king names the
Blade quest — Varatro Falls, Silvarium elders, Ofelos — and orders the coalition
to march. You pledge by class. Horns sound. Act III ends.

Scene beats:
1. Guards — Thekia vouches.
2. Recount choice — Dentros vs soldier's report.
3. Massacre testimony — Kimious, portal, ashes.
4. Vashirr's message — verbatim delivery.
5. Kaefden's response — Paladins, Blade, two fronts.
6. Class pledge — hunter / guardian / scout.
7. Mobilization — march to Aylova.

Theme: Two protagonists meet — witness and the man who forgot, then remembered.

Tone: Court drama. Earnest. Kaefden's best speechwriting.

Key dialogue to preserve:
- "I wore this crown at Oceandale's ashes seven years ago."
- "I will not let Nacastrum fall twice."

Connections: Story 9 (same king crowned); Story 15–17 (war campaign).
