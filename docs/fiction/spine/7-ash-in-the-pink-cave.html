Story 7 — Ash in the Pink Cave

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN — mountain cave, Inflame puzzle
POV Second person present
Length target 5,000 words
Cast Dead mage in fireball room; corpse trails in branches

Synopsis: Lantern light on pink crystals. Branching passages littered with
miners, bitten corpses, hanging cages. A burnt mage slumps in the corner of the
fire room — someone else sought Inflame and failed. You solve the four-symbol rite
or join the ash. The cave teaches that the quest kills indiscriminately.

Scene beats:
1. Dark entrance — swim, not fly (stalactite death if wrong).
2. Main cavern — branch exploration, symbol clues.
3. Fire room — dead mage foreshadow.
4. Puzzle — three failures burn you alive.
5. Inflame earned — fire in your hands.

Theme: Competitors on the same road. Magic has a body count.

Tone: Horror-adventure; meta humor on wrong commands optional.

Connections: Story 15 (Paladin tomb warden — same dungeon energy in SoC).
