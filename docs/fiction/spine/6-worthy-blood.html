Story 6 — Worthy Blood

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN — Silvarium, Great Tree, blood seal
POV Second person present
Length target 6,000 words
Cast Marksman; Marlux; butcher; priestess; librarian; Sylvians

Synopsis: A net trap in the forest leads to an unexpected ally — a Sylvian
marksman who frees you and names the cause: Kaefden to Kaefden. In the Great Tree
city, a resentful butcher yields Suformin's Dagger, an elk priestess blesses it,
and a vain librarian admits even elders' blood could not reopen the mage seal.
You cut your palm and learn Stonebend. Worthy blood is not ancestry alone — it is
what you will sacrifice.

Scene beats:
1. Net trap — marksman's speech.
2. Marlux on floor one — child in wartime.
3. Butcher's chest — dagger acquired amid grumbling.
4. Priestess of Suformin — prophecy.
5. Librarian — "their blood isn't worthy."
6. Fourth floor — cut, chamber, Stonebend.

Theme: Sacrifice vs. entitlement. Sylvian–mage pact under strain.

Tone: Earnest with character comedy (librarian's wolf threat, priestess's "42").

Connections: Story 13 (Silvarium elders in SoC); Story 14 (Blade as second
worthy-blood test for Ofelos).
