Story 2 — What the Barbarians Did Not Do

Field Value
Canon anchor KoN — Thekia's doubt (STORY.md §2.3)
POV Third limited — a Nacastrum citizen during banishment
Length target 4,000–5,000 words
Cast Vashirr; crowd of mages; Kaefden IV (unnamed, age 33); player's parents

Synopsis: On the night Nacastrum empties, Vashirr claims barbarians will
destroy the floating city. Portals swallow citizens by the hundred. One teacher
watches a man run toward his wife and die in blue fire. The king's true allegiance
is visible only to those who ask why a sky-city feared a northern horde that never
came.

Scene beats:
1. Alarm — blinding light in the courtyard.
2. Vashirr beside an Agromanian envoy — portals open.
3. Abductions — women and children dragged through.
4. The father runs — Vashirr kills him.
5. Scatter — last image before darkness: falling, falling.
6. Epilogue — Oceandale burns "last week"; the teacher washes ashore with no name.

Theme: Lies that save tyrants. The mystery Thekia names in the magehouse.

Tone: Tragic, tight. No humor.

Connections: Story 6 (memory restoration); Story 7 (father's corpse); entire
SoC (Vashirr's ongoing alliance).
