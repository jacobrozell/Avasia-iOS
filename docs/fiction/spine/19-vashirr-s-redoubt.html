Story 19 — Vashirr's Redoubt

Field Value
Canon anchor SoC — Vashirr's Stand (SoCVashirrStandRoom.swift)
POV Second person present
Length target 7,000 words
Cast Kaefden IV; Vashirr; Vashirr's War Mage; coalition

Synopsis: Dawn on a rocky shore. Vashirr's portal frame and banners. Kaefden
rides the line and names every crime. Vashirr unhoods — the scar. By class, you
break the ward: hunter drives the gap, guardian absorbs a bolt for the king, scout
scatters ward-stakes. You fight the War Mage. Kaefden's mages bind Vashirr in blue
crystal; the staff splinters; the portal collapses. Bound in the surf, Vashirr
whispers that the land will forget Kaefden too.

Scene beats:
1. Arrival — coalition deploys.
2. Verbal duel — Kaefden vs Vashirr (father's murder named).
3. Player beat — class-specific breach.
4. War Mage combat — authored, not grind.
5. Binding — blue crystal net, portal collapse.
6. Vashirr captured — armies scatter without portals.

Theme: Treachery ends not with a duel of kings but collective binding.

Tone: Climax earnest. Scar remembered from Story 12.

Key dialogue to preserve:
- "I earned it when you scattered the Council and murdered my people."
- "This land will forget you too, Kaefden."

Connections: Story 2, 8 (Vashirr's crimes); Story 20 (epilogue).
