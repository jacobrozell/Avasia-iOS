Story 12 — The Day Cataracta Burned

Field Value
Canon anchor SoC Act II — massacre
POV Second person present
Length target 7,000–8,000 words
Cast Vashirr; Kimious; Dentros; Agromanian horde; Paladins

Synopsis: Vashirr portals an army into the keep. Kimious dies to a dark bolt.
Dentros shoves you from the next shot and dies in your place. You fight a Grunt,
then a Warrior. Vashirr unhoods — scar, gray staff — and forces you to memorize
his message for Kaefden IV. Then he executes every captive druid. You wake in ash.

Scene beats:
1. Portal invasion — Paladins described.
2. Kimious falls.
3. Two combats — survival by class stats.
4. Dentros's death — shielding blow.
5. Vashirr's speech — message verbatim.
6. Mass execution — portal snap.
7. Awakening — alone, castle flames, genocide complete.

Theme: War's cost paid in a single courtyard. Survivor's guilt incarnate.

Tone: Earnest horror. No jokes.

Connections: Story 13 (escape); Story 4 & 5 (Dentros arc closed); Story 16
(memorial).
