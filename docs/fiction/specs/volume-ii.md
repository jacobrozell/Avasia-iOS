## II-M01 — *Kimious's Unfinished Sentence*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | SoC courtyard massacre · `SoCCataractaRooms` |
| **POV** | Third limited — King **Kimious** |
| **Length target** | 3,500–4,000 words |
| **When** | Morning of courtyard muster — hours before death |
| **Cast** | Kimious; scribe; Kimious's guard captain; distant Kimious family |

### Synopsis

Kimious drafts the speech he will never finish: hidden defenses failed, Aylova
pact must be honored, **Anula requisition** is bleeding Cataracta dry. He crosses
out a line about begging Kaefden and writes *"we stand together"* instead. Vashirr
arrives before the verb.

### Scene beats

1. **Dawn study** — ink, three drafts, crumpled paper.
2. **Requisition clipping** — pinned to wall; anger controlled.
3. **Guard captain** — reports fox sign on the road (Dentros).
4. **Speech nearly done** — interrupted by drums.
5. **Courtyard approach** — last sentence in his throat, unsaid.
6. **White space** — massacre off-page; scribe's notebook ends mid-word.

### Theme

Interrupted hope — Act II hinge in miniature.

### Tone

Earnest tragedy. **No** spectacle gore on-page.

### Connections

Story 11–12 · II-M05 · `CHARACTERS.md` Kimious.

### Key dialogue

- Draft: *"Hidden defenses are no longer enough when the sky itself—"* (cut off)

## II-M03 — *Brothers at the Pier*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | SoC Ulric/Doran chain · Anula crates |
| **POV** | Alternating — **Ulric** / **Doran** |
| **Length target** | 4,000 words |
| **When** | SoC Act I |
| **Cast** | Ulric; Doran; pier tally clerk |

### Synopsis

An **Anula crate** arrives for the forge. Ulric works the crystal into tongs
that hum; Doran watches the river and refuses to fish — the water feels counted.
Brotherly love trophy made literal: they argue, eat, and go to work anyway.

### Scene beats

1. **Delivery** — crate seal; coalition wax.
2. **Ulric** — forge song; Anula sparks blue.
3. **Doran** — pier empty; grumbles about tally clerks.
4. **Fight** — gift vs ledger; brotherly love.
5. **Reconciliation** — fish stew; forge cools.
6. **Coda** — pier cracked post-massacre; tongs survive.

### Theme

Gift → commodity transition on one pier.

### Tone

Warm, working-class.

### Connections

Story 10 · `FORESHADOWING.md` requisition.

### Key dialogue

- Doran: *"They put a price on the river. I won't sell what I can't buy back."*

## II-M06 — *Ofelos Sends an Observer*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | SoC massacre · Ofelos neutrality · [`../OFKELOS.md`](../OFKELOS.md) |
| **POV** | Third limited — neutral diplomat **Cassan** |
| **Length target** | 5,000–6,000 words |
| **When** | Day of Cataracta courtyard massacre |
| **Cast** | Cassan; Ofelos observer corps; Kimious (distant); Vashirr (aftermath) |

### Synopsis

Ofelos sends **Cassan** to observe, not intervene — neutrality as profession.
From a hill road Cassan watches portals open and understands neutrality is
**complicity** when you have eyes and no hands. Report home argues Ofelos must
enter the system or be crushed by it. Blade quest diplomacy begins here.

### Scene beats

1. **Briefing** — Ofelos council: *"Record, do not act."*
2. **Approach** — Cataracta drums; festival false calm.
3. **Observation** — massacre from distance; Cassan counts seconds.
4. **Report draft** — neutrality failed; names the cost.
5. **Council reply** — conditional alliance; Blade required.
6. **Coda** — Cassan vomits in a ditch; keeps the seal.

### Theme

Neutrality's price — Ofelos enters history.

### Tone

Cold horror, institutional guilt.

### Connections

Story 15 · `OFKELOS.md` · Story 17 Ofelos march.

### Key dialogue

- *"We remained neutral. The courtyard did not."*
