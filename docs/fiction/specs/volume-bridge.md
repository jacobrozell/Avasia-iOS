## B-01 — *Kaefden's First Ledger*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | KoN ending · Story 9b parallel |
| **POV** | Third limited — Kaefden IV |
| **Length target** | 4,000 words |
| **When** | Week after Aylova rally |
| **Cast** | Kaefden IV; Thekia; returning mages; father's ghost (memory) |

### Synopsis

Kaefden cannot sleep in the castle courtyard where his father died. Crowns feel
like theft even when earned. Thekia does not comfort him — she delivers **timber
counts**, portal shift schedules, and a list of mages who want to pretend
restoration is prayer. He learns kingship is arithmetic first, mercy second.

### Scene beats

1. **Sleepless** — pendant heavy; corpse gone but stain remains.
2. **Thekia's ledger** — stones, silver leaf, Ring hours.
3. **Petitioners** — mages argue sky vs shore academies.
4. **Kaefden decides** — public rebuild, no secret council.
5. **Night walk** — silver streets; *"Let us go"* echoed alone.
6. **Coda** — signs first tally sheet; war not over.

### Theme

Restoration is administration of grief.

### Tone

Bittersweet, institutional.

### Connections

Story 9 · Story 9b · B-03.

### Key dialogue

- Thekia: *"Crowns do not end scatter. They only decide who pays first."*
- Kaefden: *"Then I will learn the numbers before I learn the speeches."*

## B-02 — *The Mother's Thread*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | `NARRATIVE_LOGIC` #8 · game 3 mother-search |
| **POV** | Epistolary — Thekia's filed letters, registry notes, never sent |
| **Length target** | 3,500–4,500 words |
| **When** | Years 1–7 post-KoN |
| **Cast** | Thekia; clerks; Agromanian registry fragments; Kaefden (addressee, never reads) |

### Synopsis

Thekia builds a **mother-search archive** without promising rescue. Letters
gather: ground-oath clause copy; Agromanian camp lists with one name circled;
flesh-forge rumor from coalition scouts; a witness who saw portal east **before**
Oceandale burned. Each entry ends **unresolved**. **Age-era rule:** no reunion,
no body, no heroic extraction.

### Scene beats

1. **File opened** — pendant registry cross-reference.
2. **Camp list** — name partial; ink smeared.
3. **Scout rumor** — flesh-anchor stock; Thekia vomits, keeps filing.
4. **Letter to Kaefden (unsent)** — *"I will not give you hope without proof."*
5. **Year seven** — archive sealed for game 3; one red string on the wall.
6. **Coda** — spell-glass note: *"Search continues. Rescue deferred."*

### Theme

Mystery as responsibility — clues without catharsis.

### Tone

Cold document tragedy.

### Connections

Story 2 · Story 8 memory · III-M09 · I-M04.

### Key dialogue

- *"We know why she was taken. We do not know where. That is enough for now."*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | Aylova rally · `EndgameRooms` crystal tally |
| **POV** | Third limited — portal tech **Mira** (mage, not scout Mira) |
| **Length target** | 3,500 words |
| **When** | Month 1 post-KoN |
| **Cast** | Mira; Thekia; smuggler clerk; returning families |

### Synopsis

Opening Nacastrum to Aylova costs **crystal** nobody budgeted. Mira runs the Ring
crew; someone shorts the tally to skim shards. Thekia catches the theft before a
portal collapses mid-transit. Victory is counted in crystal — literally.

### Scene beats

1. **Shift** — families queue; Ring hums.
2. **Short count** — Mira's gut; charts don't match.
3. **Investigation** — wax seals, clerk's hands.
4. **Confrontation** — Thekia public; no quiet execution.
5. **Restart** — portal stable; first child runs home crying.
6. **Coda** — tally board installed forever.

### Theme

Restoration infrastructure — wonder with bookkeeping.

### Tone

Procedural hope.

### Connections

Story 9 · B-01 · W-09 Ring Road seed.

### Key dialogue

- Thekia: *"Every shard we spend is a soldier we arm later. Thieves arm no one but themselves."*

## B-05 — *Vashirr in the Cell*

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Canon anchor** | `SAGA.md` §8.5 Vashirr bound · game 3 cult seeds |
| **POV** | Third limited — prison guard **Sorin** |
| **Length target** | 4,000 words |
| **When** | Post-SoC redoubt — months after binding |
| **Cast** | Vashirr; Sorin; cult pilgrim; Kaefden (off-page) |

### Synopsis

Vashirr is bound, not executed. Cultists smuggle Many Hands pamphlets copied from
memory; they want his blessing. He reads and **refuses** — the Red Litany perverts
his doctrine. Sorin begins to think the prisoner is more dangerous alive because
**ideas** walk through bars.

### Scene beats

1. **Rotation** — scarred eye; silent cell.
2. **Pamphlet hidden in boot** — pilgrim caught.
3. **Vashirr's refusal** — *"I did not bleed men to make gods of hunger."*
4. **Sorin reports** — Kaefden orders continued imprisonment.
5. **Night** — chanting from the yard; not Vashirr's voice.
6. **Coda** — broadsheet: *"What the king bound, the pit repeats."*

### Theme

Doctrine leaks; no redemption; cult ≠ master.

### Tone

Cold, philosophical horror.

### Connections

Story 19 · III-M04 · III-M05 · `VASHIRR.md`.

### Key dialogue

- Vashirr: *"Many Hands built soldiers. Your Litany builds martyrs. I reject both endings."*
