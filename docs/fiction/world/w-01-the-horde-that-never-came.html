Synopsis

Northern clans never marched on Nacastrum — Vashirr's lie. A scout watches the
empty pass and sees sky-light to the south; understands mages fight mages.
Barbarians become footnote in another people's catastrophe.

  2. Southern flash — portals on horizon.
  3. Debate — raid or hold; hold wins.
  4. Refugees wrong direction — not north.
  5. Coda — lie spreads faster than their silence.

Theme

Thekia's doubt stands — no vindication for Vashirr.
