Synopsis

Athalos overstocked his shop because empty shelves feel like defeat. He hides
requisition clippings under the counter and sells a potion to a legion recruit
who will not survive the month. Comedy of excess with a receipt for grief.

Scene beats

  1. Inventory — absurd surplus; counts aloud.
  2. Clipping — Sylvian elder protest against requisition.
  3. Recruit — buys potion; Athalos undercharges.
  4. Night — rearranges shelves; can't fix what's coming.
  5. Coda — shop ash in Act II; one shelf somehow standing.

Theme
