Synopsis

An Anula crate arrives for the forge. Ulric works the crystal into tongs
that hum; Doran watches the river and refuses to fish — the water feels counted.
Brotherly love trophy made literal: they argue, eat, and go to work anyway.

Scene beats

  1. Delivery — crate seal; coalition wax.
  2. Ulric — forge song; Anula sparks blue.
  3. Doran — pier empty; grumbles about tally clerks.
  4. Fight — gift vs ledger; brotherly love.
  5. Reconciliation — fish stew; forge cools.
  6. Coda — pier cracked post-massacre; tongs survive.

Theme
