II-M01 — Kimious's Unfinished Sentence

Field Value

Length target 3,500–4,000 words
When Morning of courtyard muster — hours before death
Cast Kimious; scribe; Kimious's guard captain; distant Kimious family

Synopsis

Kimious drafts the speech he will never finish: hidden defenses failed, Aylova
pact must be honored, Anula requisition is bleeding Cataracta dry. He crosses
out a line about begging Kaefden and writes "we stand together" instead. Vashirr
arrives before the verb.

Scene beats

  1. Dawn study — ink, three drafts, crumpled paper.
  2. Requisition clipping — pinned to wall; anger controlled.
  3. Guard captain — reports fox sign on the road (Dentros).
  4. Speech nearly done — interrupted by drums.
  5. Courtyard approach — last sentence in his throat, unsaid.
  6. White space — massacre off-page; scribe's notebook ends mid-word.

Theme

Interrupted hope — Act II hinge in miniature.

Tone

Earnest tragedy. No spectacle gore on-page.
