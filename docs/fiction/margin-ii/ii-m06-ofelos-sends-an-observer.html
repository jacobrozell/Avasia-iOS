Synopsis

Ofelos sends Cassan to observe, not intervene — neutrality as profession.
From a hill road Cassan watches portals open and understands neutrality is
complicity when you have eyes and no hands. Report home argues Ofelos must
enter the system or be crushed by it. Blade quest diplomacy begins here.
