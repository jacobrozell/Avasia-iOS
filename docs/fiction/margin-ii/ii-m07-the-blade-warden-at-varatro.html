Galen has guarded Kaefden's Blade of Courage for forty years without seeing
it — the tomb tests worth, not rank. Coalition envoys arrive; Galen must decide if
a crowned amnesiac and a druid army deserve the first king's relic. Falls roar;
civic anchor tested.
