B-04 — Diaspora Letter from Cataracta

Field Value

POV First person letter — druid Hest to cousin in Silvarium
Length target 2,000 words
When Year 6–7 post-KoN
Cast Hest; cousin; Kimious court (distant)

Synopsis

Hest writes that peace is loud — requisition clerks, Anula crates, legion
drums practicing while fish still sell on the pier. Paladin rumors sound like
fairy tales until they don't. Last line: "If war comes, do not say we were not
warned. The fountain still takes coins. Nothing happens. We do anyway."
