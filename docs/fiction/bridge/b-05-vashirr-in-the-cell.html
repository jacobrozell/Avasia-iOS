B-05 — Vashirr in the Cell

Field Value

POV Third limited — prison guard Sorin
Length target 4,000 words
When Post-SoC redoubt — months after binding
Cast Vashirr; Sorin; cult pilgrim; Kaefden (off-page)

Synopsis

Vashirr is bound, not executed. Cultists smuggle Many Hands pamphlets copied from
memory; they want his blessing. He reads and refuses — the Red Litany perverts
his doctrine. Sorin begins to think the prisoner is more dangerous alive because
ideas walk through bars.

Scene beats

  1. Rotation — scarred eye; silent cell.
  2. Pamphlet hidden in boot — pilgrim caught.
  3. Vashirr's refusal — "I did not bleed men to make gods of hunger."
  4. Sorin reports — Kaefden orders continued imprisonment.
  5. Night — chanting from the yard; not Vashirr's voice.
  6. Coda — broadsheet: "What the king bound, the pit repeats."

Theme

Doctrine leaks; no redemption; cult ≠ master.

Tone

Cold, philosophical horror.

Connections
