# Avasia: King of Nacastrum Walkthrough

**Disclaimer:** This is just for completion of the game. This does not include every possible command or even every area.

---

## Intro

We wake up alongside a beach. We talk to this guard who tells us Oceandale was just attacked and gives us a whole bunch of unnecessary back story about a king, his two sons, the Kaefdens, the Agromanians… Whatever.

---

## Oceandale

Finally we start off in Oceandale.

- Go **WEST**. Answer Kaefden to obtain **Levitate**! Congratulations!
- Go **NORTH**; Look/Investigate; then take the sword to obtain the **Sword**! Congratulations!

That's everything we need inside Oceandale, for now anyway. Head North from the upper part of Oceandale to the Split-Path.

---

## Split-Path

This is the link between the four major areas of the game.

| Direction | Destination |
|---|---|
| North | Forest |
| East | Mountains and the cave |
| West | Path to Nacastrum |
| South | Way back to Oceandale |

It absolutely does not matter which way you choose first but, for the sake of this walkthrough, I will be going **EAST** to the mountain first.

---

## Mountain

You come across a broken bridge with a roaring chasm below. You can try cutting a tree down, or jumping in the water (which doesn't work and kills you, respectively), but I will use my handy-dandy new spell **Levitate** to cross the bridge.

Once at the foot of the mountain, you can go **NORTH**, **EAST**, or **WEST**.

1. Go **EAST** first to obtain the **Lantern** from a fox/druid. Woot!
2. You can now go **NORTH**, but for the sake of the walkthrough, I will also go **WEST**.

Go **WEST**. North the path turns to stone indicting civilization lies that way, East the mountain wall is "impenetrable", and West you see a huge tree (this is actually the main part of the forest that we will get to later). Going West, the chasm blocks our way and even if we could use levitate, the trees block a clear landing.

Go **NORTH**. You come up to a gateway enriched with blue crystals (Anula, but we will also get to that later). You can **TALK** or **APPROACH** the group of men talking. When done they begin to get ready to attack you! One turns to a bear in front of your eyes and you realize these men are a druid hunting pack.

You once again use your handy-dandy levitate spell to show them you mean no harm, because if you've been keeping up, druids and mages are both Kaefden, making them allies. You could have also shown the pack of druids your ears!

Now that all that is over, you're back where you started. Go **NORTH** inside the "divot between the rocks".

---

## Cave

First step is to "Fire up your lantern". Next you're going to need to "dive" or "swim". That puts you in the main part of the cave.

The cave is kind of tricky because its puzzle is randomly generated every play through. The good news the rooms don't switch around. So what's NORTHEAST for me, will be what's NORTHEAST for you. Like before, it doesn't matter which way you go first but I'm going to go in the order of the symbols for walkthrough purposes. Confused? Just follow along.

### West Cave

First off: Head **WEST**. Pretty straightforward, when you come to the fork in the road head **LEFT**. Here the game will give you your first symbol. **WRITE THIS DOWN.** Keep in mind that if you die or restart the game, the symbol you find here may be different.

Next up is (Drumroll please?) **Northwest Cave**.

### Northwest Cave

Long story short… There's a dead guy that was mining the shards. When you inspect his body, you find his pickaxe and the game asks you if you want to take the pickaxe. You can try and take it, but then it asks you if you want to try and mine the shards. **IF YOU MINE THE SHARDS, YOU DIE.**

If you say "No thank you, sir." You continue into the next part. This part is a bunch of text but gives you something truly valuable: the next symbol. Again write this down. Your paper should look like this: **Symbol 1 , Symbol 2**.

Next up, is my personal favorite, **Northeast Cave**.

### Northeast Cave

Again, long story short… There's some cages above you on the ceiling. Use **Levitate** to obtain the 3rd symbol. If you're keeping track at home, that's Levitate: 3 , Game: 0.

And that leaves **EAST**.

### East Cave

This one's a gimmie. Either the developers are being nice or they got lazy (#2). Take that symbol and throw at the end of your paper.

**Symbol 1 , Symbol 2 , Symbol 3 , Symbol 4.**

A sample example is `^'` , `~'` , `>'` , `;'`

After you have the four symbols, head **NORTH**.

### North Cave

When you arrive, 3 symbols seem to be prominent in the text. **TOUCH** the symbols. Pick the first one by inputting "1". A gate opens to your **NORTH**. Go **NORTH**.

### Pedestal Room

Here is where all those fancy symbols actually come into play. It saids "The symbols include: (^' ~' >' ;')". So now like before **TOUCH** the symbols.

Now whatever you have on your paper is going to be transposed from this order. For example: When you see the symbol `^'` that is a **1**. So if your paper saids `( ;' , >' , ^' , ~' )` then your input should be **4312**.

**CAREFUL.** If you get it wrong 3 times, you will die. With the correct code however, you will receive the spell **Inflame** which is just a fancy way of saying you can shoot fire out of your hands. "Flame-on!"

With this, is dumps you back out at the mountain entrance. From the mountain, go **SOUTH**, back to the Split-Path. From the Split-Path, go **NORTH** to the forest!

---

## Forest Entrance

If you haven't gotten the sword, there's not much you can do here. If you do however, use the sword to **CUT** through the tall grass and look out for pocket monsters!

Next, you spring a net trap that forces you upwards about 7 feet in the air. If you have gotten Levitate, when the man cuts you down, you levitate to your feet like a total pimp. If you haven't however, you fall 7 feet. Ouch!

The man gives you a whole bunch of lore and hullabaloo about more than half his people went north because they are scared of the Agromanians. The juicy part is when they left, they sealed away a fancy mage spell in the upper chambers of the tree. That's what we want. Go inside the tree and let's get it.

---

## Silvarum Floor #1

The only thing you can do here is **TALK** to a young boy named Marlux. He is overall pretty useless besides show that almost all of the citizen's daily lives are spent inside the tree, including learning and school. Go **UP** to the second floor.

---

## Silvarum Floor #2

Left, we got a Butcher. Right, we got an Armory. Lets go left to the Butcher first.

### Butcher

Firstly **TALK** to the leader. Then simply **EXPLAIN** yourself to Mr.Grumps. He is so glad to hear that you are getting rid of stuff from the elders, he offer to give you something the elders left behind. It's a **Dagger**!! The dagger is made from a blue material. This material is called **Anula**, the same stuff that was on the gates of Cataracta (druid city).

### Armory

We get shouted at by a guard who obviously doesn't enjoy our presence. You can try to attack her if you want or just simply **EXPLAIN** yourself once again. However, this time you don't get so lucky. She has no idea what you are talking about and throws you out anyway.

With everything we need, we venture **UP** once again.

---

## Silvarum Floor #3

Church to the left. Library to the right. Lets go **LEFT** to the church first (That kind of rhymes).

### Church

A woman is there. **TALK** to her and then you ask her about **SUFORMIN** or the **DAGGER** you just obtained. In a nutshell, she saids the dagger was given to you for a reason and she expects great things from you. Let's leave and go to the Library.

### Library

This is the most important room in all of the tree. You can explain your task or ask about the mages. Read carefully. The hints he provides are crucial to obtaining the spell hidden in the tree. When asked about the mages, the librarian eventually saids "It seems that their blood isn't worthy!" This is what you came for. **LEAVE** and then head **UP**.

---

## Silvarum Floor #4

You arrive and see a huge seal. Two daggers overlap in the middle with blood dripping from them. "You get a hint, and you get a hint….etc." It asks you what you want to do and you **CUT** yourself with the **DAGGER**. You can cut yourself on your hand, leg, or neck.

- **Neck** will kill you, obviously; which means you have to do the ENTIRE game again.
- **Hand** or **Leg** however, will cause blood to drip to on the seal below you, revealing a hidden entrance!

Long story short, you obtain the spell **Stonebend**! Congratulations! Then it plops you back in front of the "Great Deku Tree". I mean Sylvarum. Head **SOUTH** to go back to the Split-Path. From here head **WEST** to Nacastrum (well, almost).

---

## Western Road

North takes you to the beach, where you can fish but, we will get to that much later. East is the Path to Nacastrum. Go **EAST**.

---

## Path to Nacastrum

### RoadBlock #1

The first "roadblock" (as we will call them) is an attack from a group of Agromanians. You get two chances to kill them. If you enter anything other than **Inflame** the first time you are granted with one more chance, if you still don't enter **Inflame** or don't have the spell Inflame, they overrun you and kill you. If you enter **Inflame** and have the spell Inflame, you kill them all and continue to roadblock #2.

### RoadBlock #2

A big stone pillar blocks your way. Use your fancy **Stonebend** spell to blast a tunnel right through. Continue to roadblock #3.

### RoadBlock #3

You come across the same broken bridge you saw earlier at the mountain. Crazy right? This one seems a little tricky and you can levitate right across the chasm like you did before but, the correct answer is to **JUMP** or **DIVE** into the water. What killed you earlier turns out to be the correct answer here. Nonetheless, this seems to trigger you back to reality.

You've finally arrived! Nacastrum, the City of the Mage lies before you! Or not. You have no idea how to get up there or what this strange monument is used for. Looks like we are going all the way to back to Oceandale to ask the Old Mage for help (remember her?).

---

## Escorting the Old Mage

Go all the way back to the Old Mage's house. She randomly decides that "Oh yeah, hey, I have this super crazy Staff of Malkos in here." She informs you that that monument is a teleporter and the staff will activate it. She decides she will follow you back to Nacastrum.

The game basically gives you directions based off what directional options have been taken away. Take her back to Nacastrum's teleporter and you get your memory back while teleporting back to Nacastrum.

Long story short, you arrive at Nacastrum and the Old Mage reveals her name is **Thetkia**. You activate a portal in the center of Nacastrum that goes to Aylova, Kaefden's capital. You and Thekia arrive there and shout an SOS to come and help rebuild Nacastrum. Within minutes, hundreds of Mages and other kin come to help restore Nacastum. Thetkia looks to you and thanks you and reminds you that your quest still isn't over. The game ends with "Let us go, my king." Looks like the protagonist became the new King of Nacastrum.

---

## Fishing

At the beach, you can go to the hut and take the fishing rod. Then head **NORTH** to begin fishing. There are a total of 12 possible things you can fish up. Good luck!

---

## Extra Lore

The roadblocks weren't just set up for no reason; **Vashir**, the old king of Nacastrum, set up these roadblocks to prevent mages from entering Nacastrum. He grew too cocky over years of being king and he believed his roadblocks would suffice. Boy, was he wrong.

Vashirr is loosely based off of the spanish conquistador Cortez.
