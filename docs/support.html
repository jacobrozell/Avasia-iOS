Getting help

Avasia is a single-player, offline text adventure. Most issues are resolved by checking in-app Settings (text pacing, sound) or starting a new game from the saga menu.

Saved progress, achievements, and trophies are stored on your device. Uninstalling the app removes that data.

Contact

For bug reports or questions, reach out through Buy Me a Coffee (message button on the profile). You can also open an issue on the GitHub repository if you use GitHub.
