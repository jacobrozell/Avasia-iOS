Overview

Avasia is an offline text adventure. We designed it to work without an account, analytics SDK, or ad network.

Data stored on your device

The app saves game progress, achievements, trophies, and settings locally on your iPhone or iPad using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to us.

Network use

Gameplay does not require the internet. If you tap Support links (such as Buy Me a Coffee), your browser opens that third-party site under their privacy terms.

Children

Avasia does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Because no data is sent to our servers, there is nothing for us to store or sell.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The “last updated” date below reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developers listed in the in-app Credits screen, or via the support page.
